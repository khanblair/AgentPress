# AgentPress Brand Guidelines

## Visual Identity
- **Primary Color:** #1A1A2E (Deep Navy)
- **Secondary Color:** #16213E (Dark Blue)
- **Accent Color:** #E94560 (Crimson Red)
- **Text Color:** #EAEAEA (Off-White)
- **Background Color:** #0F3460 (Deep Sea)
- **Typography:** Calibri, Arial, or Helvetica

## Tone of Voice
- **Primary Tone:** Professional, authoritative, and data-driven.
- **Secondary Tone:** Forward-thinking and innovative.
- **Audience:** Enterprise leaders and market analysts.

## Mandatory Elements
- **Logo Placement:** Top-left or top-center (Slide 1).
- **Confidentiality:** All documents must include "CONFIDENTIAL - AgentPress Internal" in the footer.
